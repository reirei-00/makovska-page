Viktoriia Makovska

I am a PhD student at the UCU Applied Sciences Faculty. My PhD topic is Forgetting Machines: Responsibility by Design in Machine Unlearning.

I hold an M.Sc. in Data Science with honors from Ukrainian Catholic University (2025), and a Master's degree in Radio Engineering from Kharkiv National University of Radioelectronics (2015).

I previously worked at the French startup Artur'In, progressing from testing and backend development to a team lead role coordinating developers and QA engineers. I have been involved in the Responsible AI for Ukraine program, focusing on ethical data practices and participatory data modeling.

My interests sit at the intersection of sociology, ethics, and computer science, with an emphasis on responsible AI and how technology shapes society.
